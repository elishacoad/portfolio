Overview

Marky is a tool that allows you to post a month of social media content in 5 minutes. I've been onboard since the beginning, leading the product design. While still a small startup, big things are ahead. We already have over a thousand sign ups with more coming in everyday. Check it out for yourself at mymarky.ai!

The Mission

My focus is to create a simple and intuitive user experience that makes running an effective social media marketing campaign accessible to everyone. Because of this, the onboarding has been fiercely simplified to help people use Marky with as little friction as possible. So far it's been a big success and people have been loving Marky.
