Hello! I’m Elisha, a designer by day and a tired designer by night. I'm passionate about crafting user-centric digital experiences that enhance people's lives. Leveraging my background in programming and branding, I combine my skills to create comprehensive interface solutions for companies in the tech industry.

I also love nerding out about classical literature, 3D modeling, and board games.

mission

I want technology that gives rather than takes. I want software that builds bridges rather than burns them. I want products that are made for humans rather than machines.

And I want to do this in a beautiful, considerate, and purposeful way. That is why I’m a designer. Sure, I love the strategy, creativity, and meticulousness that come with the job. But at the end of the day, the true measure of success is how much my work empowers others.

background

I grew up in the beautiful woodlands near Coeur d'Alene, Idaho. From an early age, I was fascinated by technology and what it enables people to create. I started programming small games and building websites, which eventually led to freelance work as a software developer and graphic designer. Entering college, I combined my passion for the visual and the technical, getting a degree in interface design.

I now use my skills to create beautiful yet practical designs that are built with user's needs first-and-foremost.
